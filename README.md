# 🦉 Math Stars

A friendly little math trainer for kids to practice the **little 1×1** — addition, subtraction, multiplication and division with numbers from 1 to 10.

**Try it here: https://thomassymann.github.io/kids-math/**

No account, no ads, no tracking. It runs entirely in your browser and even works offline once installed.

## How to use it

1. Pick what to practice with the buttons at the top:
   - **+** Addition (up to 10 + 10)
   - **−** Subtraction (never goes below zero)
   - **×** Multiplication (up to 10 × 10)
   - **÷** Division (always comes out even — no remainders)
   - **🎲** Mixed — a random mix of all four
2. Solve the question using the big number pad and press **✔**.
3. Got it right? You earn a ⭐ and the next question appears. Got it wrong? Try again — after three tries the app shows the answer and moves on.

The scoreboard at the top counts correct answers ✔, wrong answers ✘ and your current streak 🔥. The score resets every time you start the app, so every session is a fresh game.

## Add it to your phone like a real app

Installing puts a Math Stars icon on the home screen, opens it full-screen without browser bars, and makes it work without an internet connection.

### iPhone / iPad (iOS)

1. Open https://thomassymann.github.io/kids-math/ in **Safari**.
2. Tap the **Share** button (the square with the arrow pointing up).
3. Scroll down and tap **Add to Home Screen**.
4. Tap **Add**. The Math Stars icon now appears on your home screen.

### Android

1. Open https://thomassymann.github.io/kids-math/ in **Chrome**.
2. Tap the **⋮** menu in the top-right corner.
3. Tap **Add to Home screen** (on some devices it says **Install app**).
4. Confirm with **Install** / **Add**.

That's it — happy practicing! 🌟
